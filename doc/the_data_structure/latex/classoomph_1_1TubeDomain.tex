\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Tube as a domain. The entire domain must be defined by a \mbox{\hyperlink{classoomph_1_1GeomObject}{Geom\+Object}} with the following convention\+: zeta\mbox{[}0\mbox{]} is the coordinate along the centreline, zeta\mbox{[}1\mbox{]} is the theta coordinate around the tube wall and zeta\mbox{[}2\mbox{]} is the radial coordinate. The outer boundary must lie at zeta\mbox{[}2\mbox{]} = 1. 

The domain is parametrised by five macro elements (a central box surrounded by four curved elements) in each of the nlayer slices. The labelling of the macro elements is as follows with the zeta\mbox{[}0\mbox{]} coordinate coming out of the page.
